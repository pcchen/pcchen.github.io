## Homework
### How to use NotebookLM to do your homework.
* Use one of your google account to login into https://notebooklm.google.com/ 
* Create a new notebook, and upload one chapter's PDF (e.g. Chapter5.pdf) as one of the source. 
* Rename the notebook as "Your_Student_ID: Chapter X".
* Generate 心智圖，學習卡，測驗 and use them to help you understand the concepts in this chapter.
* Do you homework problems by yourself.
* Use AI to generate the solutions to the homework problems. Compare to your solutions. Ask AI questions if you cannot understand.
* After you complete understand the solution (meaning you can do the problem by yourself.) Add the solution to the source.
* On the due day, share the Notebook to 
    * pcchen@gapp.nthu.edu.tw
    * ccy2003@gapp.nthu.edu.tw
* **You don't need to hand in homework written on paper**.
