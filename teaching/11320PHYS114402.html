Textbooks#

  • Physics for Scientists and Engineers & with Modern Physics 10/e (Serway and Jewett)

In this semester we plan to cover following topics

Part IV: ELECTRICITY AND MAGNETISM#

  1. Electric Fields.

  2. Continuous Charge Distributions and Gauss’s Law.

  3. Electric Potential.

  4. Capacitance and Dielectrics.

  5. Current and Resistance.

  6. Direct Current Circuits.

  7. Magnetic Fields.

  8. Sources of the Magnetic Field.

  9. Faraday’s Law.

  10. Inductance.

  11. Alternating Current Circuits.

  12. Electromagnetic Waves.

Part V: LIGHT AND OPTICS.#

  1. The Nature of Light and the Laws of Geometric Optics.

  2. Interference of Light Waves.

  3. Diffraction Patterns and Polarization.

Part VI: MODERN PHYSICS.#

  1. Relativity.

  2. Introduction to Quantum Physics.

  3. Quantum Mechanics.

  4. Atomic Physics.
